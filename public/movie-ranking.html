Must Watch movies according to Dishika

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Devil Wears Prada

This is the ultimate feel-good girly movie you can ever watch. If you are a typical girl who loves fashion and dressing up, this movie is for you. It shows how an average day is like in a top fashion magazine with a stuck-up boss who wants nothing but perfection.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

This movie is one of the most liberating movies of all time. It reminds you that life is all about doing the things that you really love before it is too late. The movie throws light on the journey of 3 best friends completely different from each other all set for a road trip together. By the end, they learn major life lessons that change their lives for the better.

Maze Runner

This movie is a trilogy. It is the perfect series to watch if you want a really good thriller. It keeps you hooked throughout the movie waiting eagerly for what comes next. This movie surrounds a maze which opens every day with new routes and about 25 people stuck on one side of the maze in desperate need of an escape route.
